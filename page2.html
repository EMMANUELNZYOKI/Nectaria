Public Campaigns

A public awareness campaign on distracted driving focuses on educating the public, especially young drivers, about its dangers. Using social media, TV ads, billboards, and community events, the campaign aims to spread impactful messages, share personal stories, and promote safe driving habits to reduce accidents and save lives. »

SafetyplusLimited 13 December 2015

Road safety and awareness

Equipping students with road safety knowledge through school clubs involves engaging, hands-on activities and consistent education. Clubs can host workshops with local traffic officers, create interactive sessions on safe driving, organize safety drills, and launch peer-led campaigns. Involving students in creating awareness posters, videos, and social media content fosters a deeper understanding and commitment to road safety. By integrating these activities into school clubs, students become proactive advocates for safe driving within their communities.»

SafetyplusLimited 13 December 2015

Community Engagement

Community engagement for road safety encompasses a range of activities aimed at involving residents and organizations in promoting safe behaviors on the roads. This includes organizing events such as safety walks, bike rodeos, and car seat clinics to educate and practice safe practices. Partnering with schools, businesses, and community organizations helps spread awareness through workshops, training sessions, and public forums. Recognition programs and volunteer initiatives further encourage participation and commitment to creating a safer environment for all road users. »
