Attachment and Acomodation

-Our attachment accommodation is usually a short-term rental service for a few months of about two to three months during the attachment period.
If the company brings in attached student for a longer period of more than three months the accommodation contract can be extended for upto six months when the attached student has been promoted to internship.
-NB:Article 15(1) of the CIT acts states that a company may include fees related to the accommodation of an employee in tax detuctable cost.These applies to both rent and fees for administarative services or utilities.
Of course, the cost of the accommodation must be related to obtain income or maintaining and securing the source of this income. »
