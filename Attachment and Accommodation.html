ATTACHMENT AT SAFETYPLUS

Introduction The industrial attachment exercise is a vital part of higher education, especially in Kenya. It provides students with a real-world organizational environment to develop essential skills for their professional growth. Our unique Industrial Attachment Program helps students discover and advance their careers through well-structured, credit-bearing employment experiences, combining theory with practice. The program includes professional training from start to finish, allowing students to apply academic skills in relevant industries and gain insights into their operations. We recognize the importance of exploring career interests, learning new skills, gaining job-related experience, building professional networks, and understanding workplace expectations. Students are expected to apply their school knowledge and develop their competencies during the attachment. In response to increased demand, we have established a dedicated department to coordinate the program, ensuring personalized attention to all participants. Currently, we accept students in the following courses: Accounting & Finance Procurement & Logistics Statistics & ICT-based Courses Mass Media, Journalism, PR & Applied Communication Hospitality Courses

Attachment and Acomodation

Our attachment accommodation is usually a short-term rental service for about two to three months during the attachment period.
If the company brings in an attached student for a period longer than three months, the accommodation contract can be extended for up to six months when the attached student has been promoted to an internship.
NB: Article 15(1) of the CIT Act states that a company may include fees related to the accommodation of an employee in tax detuctable cost.These applies to both rent and fees for administarative services or utilities.
Of course, the cost of the accommodation must be related to obtain income or maintaining and securing the source of this income. »

Hands-On Experience

This is any activity of the student's major performed that relates to an industry or job-specific responsibilities.
Students gaining hands-on experience in their field is a great way to prepare for a job, as they can learn the skills and procedures that can help them succeed as professionals

  • Attached student can start earning hands-on experience as early as undergraduate level which can set you apart from other candidates in a job search who might not have the same depth of experience
  • It allows candidates to build the skills and expertise before entering a industry as professional
  • It is crusial to people who want to enter specialised field that involves advance skills because they can learn nuances and protocol that their desired career calls for
  • Most employers ask for candidates to have some professional experience before applying for an open position, so gaining hands-on experience can prepare you to apply for new jobs
